ClipTend

A self-hosted tool that scans public Twitch clips and reposts trending ones to your own TikTok account.

Terms of Service Privacy Policy

ClipTend runs entirely on your own computer. It has no backend server, and its author does not collect or store your data.

Questions? Contact austin.meier@hotmail.com.

ClipTend is an independent project and is not affiliated with Twitch or TikTok.
